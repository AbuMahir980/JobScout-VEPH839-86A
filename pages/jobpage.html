UI & UX Designer

Microsoft
Redmond, Washington.

$5,000 - $10,000/month

Posted two days ago, ends by August 8

Job Description

  • We are searching for an experienced designer that will design an E- commerce platform where users can either upload their goods to to be sold or go there to purchase items.
  • Payment can be made on this platform with credit cards and bank transfer
  • Buyer and sellers will have the chance of chatting in a private chart room
  • Both buyer and sells can drop the review of their experience on this platform
  • They also get to ranks depending on their interaction with this page and their reward is a shopping coupon

Requirements

  • Minimum of 1 year working experience.
  • Must have designed a functional website before now
  • Must have good command of English
  • The designer must able to work in a team
  • Designer must know how to use figma, sketch, Adobe XD
  • Designer must be conversant with auto - layout and constraints for responsive design

Responsibility

  • Applicant is expected to carry out user research, i.e collecting information about the targeted audience about their needs, pain point, gain, opportunities and what they would want us to include in our product.
  • The UI/UX designer is expected to create a detailed user journey map, user flow and a detailed case study
  • The Designer is expected to improve our current design to enhance user experience
  • The designer is expected to work closely with other teams to ensure that the product is developed and launched successfully
