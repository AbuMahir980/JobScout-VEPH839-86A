Privacy & Safety

  1. At Job Scout, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy and Security policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using our job search platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

    • Information Collection:

      • We collect personal information, such as your name, contact details, work experience, and educational background, to provide you with a personalized and effective job search experience. We may also collect non-personal information, such as your IP address, browser type, and device information, to enhance our platform's performance and security.

    • Use of Information:

      • We use your personal information to match you with relevant job opportunities, communicate with you regarding your applications, and improve our platform's functionality. We may also use aggregated and anonymized data for analytics and research purposes.

    • Data Storage and Protection:

      • We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We store your data on secure servers and regularly update our security protocols to protect against data breaches.

    • Third-Party Services:

      • We may utilize third-party services, such as analytics tools and job posting platforms, to enhance our platform's functionality. These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We are not responsible for the practices or content of these external websites or services.

    • Data Sharing:

      • We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information with trusted partners, such as employers and recruiters, to facilitate job applications and opportunities. We ensure that these partners adhere to strict privacy standards.

    • Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

      • We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to personalize your experience, analyze usage patterns, and improve our services. You have the option to manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, but note that certain features of our platform may be affected.

    • User Control and Access:

      • You have the right to review, update, or delete your personal information in your account settings. You may also request a copy of your data or object to the processing of your information in certain circumstances. Please contact our support team for assistance with these requests.

    • Updates to Privacy and Security Policy:

      • We may update this Privacy and Security policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review the policy regularly. Continued use of our platform after any modifications constitutes your consent to the updated policy.

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or the security of your data, please contact our privacy team at [privacy email]. We are committed to protecting your information and ensuring a safe and transparent user experience.
